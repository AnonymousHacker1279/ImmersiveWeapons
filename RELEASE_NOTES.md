This major update ports to MC 1.21.11.

### Feature Changes / Additions

- Pikes have been removed since the vanilla spear is too similar
    - New spear tiers have been added as a replacement
- Added ambience loops/additions/mood sounds to Tiltros biomes
- Bear trap and landmine camouflage models have been updated
- Super Hans now has self-preservation skills when the player is moving at sufficient speeds and is holding a spear
- Ventus and Tesla recipes now require a smithing template, and are upgraded from Netherite gear
- Replaced Void armor equip sounds
